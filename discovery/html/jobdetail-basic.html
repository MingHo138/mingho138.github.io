Job Description

Responsibilities Include:

  • Assist Digital Solutions department in all operation duties;
  • Be responsible for updating project files, data entry, filing and scanning;
  • Coordinate with different departments and provide admin support to Production Team;
  • Support in account servicing and quality assurance;
  • Prepare quotation and finance reports;
  • Handle ad hoc assignments when necessary

Who You Are:

  • Higher diploma / University graduate;
  • Aggressive, self-motivated and result oriented;
  • Good command of English and Chinese;
  • Good communication and interpersonal skills

Company Overview

ESD Services Limited is a joint venture between CK Hutchison (formerly Hutchison Whampoa) and Hewlett-Packard which both have been ranked on the Forbes Global 2000 List. We have been recognized as a pioneer in the industry by establishing the world’s first bilingual e-government portal “ESDlife” for Hong Kong government. Today, ESDlife has transitioned and developed into a full-fledged online lifestyle media and e-business company with our flagship brand, ESDlife Wedding, Hong Kong’s most popular wedding planning website. Together with our other brands, such as ESDlife Family and healthyD, we are now one of the largest and most diversified e-business in Hong Kong. ESDlife is all about people. We not only aim to enrich people’s lives through digital technology, we are also empowered by our amazing team members. Here, you’ll be different because of incredible culture, you’ll be challenged because of valuable opportunities, you’ll be inspired because of brilliant creativeness and you’ll be proud because of being one of us. If you have the spunk and brains to make a difference, come join us now!
