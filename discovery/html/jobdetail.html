Job Description

Responsibilities:

  • Attend site safety walk and safety meeting
  • Prepare and conduct health and safety document
  • Prepare and conduct health and safety training
  • Any other duties as instructing by supervisors

 

Requirements:

  • Safety and Health Supervisor (Construction) Course Certificate is a must, which if runs by OSHC or CICTA is an advantage;
  • Vaid green card is a must;
  • Holder of “First Aid” Certificate preferred (St. John or Red Cross);
  • Proficient in MS Words, Excel and PowerPoint;
  • Good communication and interpersonal skills

 

We offer public holiday, double pay, performance bonus and Successful Career Path and Future!

Interested parties please send full resume with expected salary to careers @keio.com.hk
