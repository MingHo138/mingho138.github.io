Job Description

About Our Company

At jobsDB, we are committed and focused on “Improving lives through better careers”.

With rapid growth in our company business, we are expanding our sales team to acquire more sales talents with the passion to join our growing company. jobsDB is definitely a great place for you to grow and to explore your career under a driven and supportive culture.

About Your Role

1. Develop strong working relations with our Recruitment Agency customers to enhance customer satisfaction and retention

2. Provide solutions through face to face visits, accounts review and reporting, product forums and other account management activities

3. Understand our clients' business, help to create mutually beneficial outcomes as well as provide insights and recommendations

4. Manage annual contract negotiations and resolve challenges in a tactful way

5. With end in mind of achieving long term value creation

6. Address clients and sales' feedback to key support functions such as Product and Trade Marketing

Skills & Experience

The ideal candidate would have:

  • Minimum 3 years solid sales experience in accounting management
  • Aggressive attitude, hardworking and eager to learn with a positive mindset
  • Must have experience in achieving agreed monthly sales target
  • High proficiency in both spoken and written English and Chinese
  • Strong presentation, communication and interpersonal skills
  • Proactive, forward thinking and ability to adapt to changes easily

Benefits

  • 5 day work week
  • Medical insurance
  • Educational allowance
