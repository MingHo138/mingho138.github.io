Job Description

Our client is a wholly owned subsidiary of a PRC based mainboard listed company in Hong Kong.  Currently she is sincerely seeking for a high calibre candidate to fulfill the following posts:

 

Senior Accountant/ Accountant

 

Responsibilities:

  • Prepare group consolidation
  • Handle business/ field analysis
  • Analyse performance and profitability of projects
  • Prepare budget and cash forecast
  • Liaise with auditors and professionals
  • Assist in the publication of the company's announcement or circular
  • Assist in ad-hoc projects

 

Requirements:

  • University degree holder
  • Professional qualification HKICPA/ ACCA or equivalent
  • 3 to 4 years relevant working experience, preferably with audit exposure
  • Experience in working in mainland China will be a big plus
  • Proficiency in Cantonese & English; Fluent Mandarin is highly preferred
  • Initiative, independent and detail minded
  • Candidates with lesser experience and qualification will be considered as Accountant

 

Competitive salary package and fringe benefits are offered to the right candidate

For more information, please visit our website at www.amacjob.com
