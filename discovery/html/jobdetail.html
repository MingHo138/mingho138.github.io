Job Description

Human Resources Office

Responsibilities: 

  • Supervise a team of staff to administer payroll and daily correspondences.
  • Collaborate with different stakeholders in streamlining work process to achieve organizational goals.
  • Assist in the development of HR policies and procedures to ensure consistency and compliance with legislation.
  • Prepare various HR management reports.
  • Lead and support ad-hoc projects as assigned from time to time.

 Requirements:

  • University graduate in Human Resource Management or Computer Science.
  • Five subjects, including Mathematics, Chinese Language and English Language (“Syllabus B” before 2007), at level 3 or Grade D, or above in HKCEE, or Five subjects including Mathematics, Chinese Language and English Language, at level 3 or above and 2 other subjects in HKDSE (e.g. new senior secondary subjects at level 3 or above, Applied learning subjects at attained level or above, and other language subjects at grade D or above ); or equivalent qualification assessed by the Hong Kong Council for Accreditation of Academic and Vocational Qualifications.
  • About 4 to 5 years of working experience, preferably from NGO sector.
  • Fluency in written and spoken Chinese and English.
  • Well-versed in employment-related ordinances in Hong Kong.
  • Good at the application of MS Word and Excel. Experience in using Visio is an added advantage. 
  • Good communication and leadership skills; good at time management.
  • Positive work attitude, well-organized, detailed-minded, presentable, able to work under pressure and be a team player.
  • Member of IHRM is an advantage.  

Please apply with application letter and CV, with present and expected salary, to Ms Tse, Human Resources Manager, Human Resources Office, Hong Kong Christian Service, 7/F, 33 Granville Road, Tsim Sha Tsui, Kowloon, Hong Kong by Nov 4, 2018. Applicants should consider their applications unsuccessful if not invited for interview within two months.  The information provided by the applicants would be kept confidential and would only be used for the purposes relating to the recruitment of the respective vacant posts.
